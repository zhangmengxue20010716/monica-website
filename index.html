About Me

Shanghai-based creator & China marketing expert

I've always been drawn to what lies beyond the familiar. I studied in France and Spain, backpacked through 23 countries, learned to navigate new cultures long before I knew how to turn that into a career. Somewhere along the way, I realised that understanding people — really understanding them — is the most powerful marketing skill there is.

Today, I bring that perspective to everything I do. With hands-on experience at TikTok and ByteDance, a Master's from Tongji University, and 30+ brand collaborations under my belt — from Huawei and BYD to TOEFL and HiPP — I specialise in helping overseas brands and creators build a real, resonant presence on Xiaohongshu. Not just followers. Real connection.

I speak Chinese, English, and Spanish (and a little German — work in progress). Every project I take on gets the full weight of that cross-cultural lens.
